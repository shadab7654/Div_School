About Institution

DECCAN VALLEY INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

Leaders For Tomorrow

Established in 2019, Deccan Valley International School (DVIS) is a Hassan-based co-ed private day school. Conceptualised by the Vidyunmala Educational trust, DVIS was led by well-known educationists who were in education domain from a decade. DVIS has adopted advanced methods and norms of teaching putting in place an integrated system of learning.

Our key features:

* Montessori/play way method
* To develop the analytical and creative innovative intellect
* Counselling services for grooming the IQ and EQ of students
* All-round development of our child through curriculum and co-curricular activities
* Hands on approach for learning

WHY DVIS?

*We maintain a climate of human respect and dignity
*Green campus with peaceful environment
*We teach with the amalgamation of upgraded teaching methods
*Students will learn life skill activities such as teamwork, leadership, accountability, patience, and self-confidence through sports and even they learn to face real-life challenges.
